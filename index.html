MALDIVES YOGA RETREAT

If you are looking for an escape to reconnect with your true-self , to recharge and to hit that pause button. Escape to a paradise island in  one of the most beautiful locations on earth! Nothing can beat yoga , white sandy beaches , coconut trees , sunsets and snorkeling in Indian Ocean! Our days will start with meditations, energizing yoga and end with mindset coaching sessions which will help you to discover your true desires and level up in all parts of life!

And what do you do in between?! Swimming in the Indian Ocean , chasing dolphins, meeting new people and learning their traditions , snorkeling or simply enjoying a book on the white beaches and soaking all the Vitamin D!
